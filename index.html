Welcome to my project CarDVR

This is my "Hello World" Android project, which is used to record video while you're driving a car.

This is just a beginning

This project contains two activities now, one is for recording video, the other is a list view of saved videos, main complete features are listed as below:

  • 1. Record video with camera(Camera, MediaRecorder, SurfaceHolder)
  • 2. Save video files on SDCard
  • 3. Get thumbnail of video for list view(query, getThumbnail)
